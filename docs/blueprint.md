# **App Name**: Nexus Mandate

## Core Features:

- Client Profile Management: Create, view, and edit detailed client profiles including industry types and key contact information.
- Mandate Creation & Tracking: Define and track job mandates with specific roles, required headcount, associated fees, and progress through customizable workflow stages.
- Commercial Terms Administration: Administer and link specific commercial terms or contracts to individual clients or mandates.
- Active Mandates Dashboard: Provide a centralized dashboard view of all active mandates, their current stage, and key performance indicators.
- Search & Filter Functionality: Enable efficient searching and filtering across clients and mandates based on various attributes like industry, mandate status, or terms.
- AI-Powered Mandate Summary Tool: Generate concise summaries of mandate progress and status updates using an AI tool, tailored for client communications or internal reports.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color scheme: Light. Inspired by professionalism and reliability, reflecting a structured corporate environment.
- Primary color: A deep, authoritative corporate blue (#1F5CCC) for primary interactive elements, headings, and key information, signifying trust and stability.
- Background color: A very light, clean blue-gray (#EEF1F4) providing a spacious and professional backdrop that is easy on the eyes for data-dense displays.
- Accent color: A subtle, muted green (#44AB68) used sparingly for positive status indicators, confirmations, and elements that require gentle emphasis.
- Headline and Body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its exceptional legibility, modern aesthetic, and versatility across various text sizes, ensuring clarity in data presentation.
- Use a set of simple, crisp line-art icons that maintain a clean and professional appearance, consistent with corporate branding.
- Emphasize clear hierarchy, ample whitespace, and grid-based structures to organize complex data, ensuring a professional and easy-to-navigate user experience.
- Incorporate subtle, functional animations for transitions and feedback (e.g., loading states, form submissions) to enhance perceived responsiveness without being distracting.
